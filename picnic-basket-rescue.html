Mohile Family Bears

Picnic Basket Rescue

The flagship adventure. Fast. Emotional. Big villain. Big win.

The Story

Dark Ranger Magnus has sealed the Royal Picnic Basket and trapped Baby Cubby inside the Forbidden Forest. Bruno Bear must race through honey trails, defeat elite forest rangers, collect Courage Crystals, and face Magnus himself before sunset.

Main Villain: Ranger Magnus

Magnus is not a normal ranger. He controls the forest gates, commands the ranger patrols, and guards the final golden basket fortress. The final battle decides whether the Mohile Family Bears become legends.

Arcade Speed

Fast movement, chase pressure, power-ups, combo scoring.

Boss Battle

Final showdown against Magnus with cinematic win sequence.

Emotional Ending

Save Cubby, unlock the family victory celebration.
