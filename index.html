Picnic Basket Rescue

Deep inside Honey Forest, Baby Cubby followed the smell of sandwiches and honey cake. But the forest rangers locked the picnic basket area. Only Bruno Bear can save Cubby.

Your job: Move fast like Pac-Man. Eat every honey dot in the maze. Avoid the rangers. Grab red Brave Honey to turn the chase around. When the trail is clear, run to the picnic basket and rescue Cubby.
